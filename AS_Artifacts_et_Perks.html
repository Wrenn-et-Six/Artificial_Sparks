Artifacts

- Unique items that can be found or given to the players.

Journal of the Ceased

This artifact can be given by the players when visiting the oracle Chisis, a classic feminist Naga in the temple of Kefnet. When asked for information on the planes, the plane locked oracle only knows one thing that could possibly help this trio that will soon venture further than she could even imagine, a journal passed between three 'walkers that call Amonkhet one of their homes.

Each page is titled with a different name for a plane, with three distinct handwritings as paragraphs of text. The first is hastily written and usually offers a brief and almost unreadable description of a specific plane, each rushed block signed, Samut. The second paragraph usually offers up a specific unique race native to the new plane and whether that plane has any sands (which is usually dissapointed if it doesn't). Along with these, a rough sketch of one of these people of the race, all signed Basri Ket. Lastly, an angrily written description of the reptilians native to any plane, and is even missing on certain planes, seemingly on ones where dragons don't exist, simply signed, Vol.

There are dozens of copies of this book because the Vizier has two mummified servants copying the script perfectly, allowing her to hand out the copy given to the players.

Effects - When pulled out on insight checks about a new world, it can grant advantage on these rolls.

A Lazotep Pickaxe

Found at the top of a pile of Lazotep mined by Heptor's servant mummies at Heptor's pit, this carved wooden hanndle hoists a large shimmering, but dark blue pick-axe head atop it. When it's magical properties are glimpsed, it shimmers a brilliant fiery blue, with a slight heat to it as well.

Effects - Must be properly attuned - When a foe is struck with the pick, they must make a CON(7) save or be instantly incarcerated into a sarcophagus made of the valuable blue Amonkhet mineral. Otherwise, it deals 1d4 (1d6 Versatile) piercing damage.

Perks

- Unique abilities that can be discovered by the players.

Zendikari Grit (Unique to the Goblin)

Effects - Once per long rest as a bonus action, rocks can be found and eaten, healing the player by 1d4+CON. This effect can give temporary hitpoints.

Kaladeshi Resiliance (Unique to the Aetherborn)

Effects - Once per long rest as a bonus action, while in liminal spaces, healing the player by 1d4+CON.

Loxodon's Durability (Unique to the Loxodon)

Effects - Once per long rest as a bonus action, while in a quiet contemplation, healing the player by 1d4+CON.

Demon-locked Planeswalking

Effects - One per long rest, planeswalking can be achieved by focusing on a target plane from the selection of connected planes and rolling a d6 die. With a check, this roll can be rolled with advantage (2d6's) with the player (or DM) explaining the target location on their homeplane.

Targets -

  • 1. Innistrad
  • 2. Kaladesh
  • 3. Amonkhet
  • 4. Ravnica
  • 5. Kaldheim
  • 6. Zendikar

    • Fire Giant's Intuition

    Effects - Player may add +2 attack or Spell Save to any spell or attack that involves Fire abilities in some way.
